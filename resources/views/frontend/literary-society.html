About Literary Society

Promoting literature, language and creative expression.

Literary Society gives students a platform to participate in writing, reading, poetry, essay competitions, book discussions, literary talks and creative expression.

It helps students improve vocabulary, communication, imagination, presentation skills and interest in language and literature.
