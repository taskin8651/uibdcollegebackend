About Dramatics Society

Encouraging theatre, creativity and cultural confidence.

Dramatics Society provides students opportunities to explore theatre, acting, script reading, stage performance, cultural presentation and creative expression.

It helps students build confidence, teamwork, communication skills, stage discipline and personality development through cultural activities.

Theatre performance Stage confidence Creative expression Team coordination Cultural participation Personality development

Dramatics Society objectives and student benefits.
