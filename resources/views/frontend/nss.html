Welcome to NSS

National Service Scheme Unit of B.D. College, Patna.

The National Service Scheme is a public service programme under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. It aims to inculcate social responsibility, civic awareness and community service among youth.

NSS volunteers work through activities and initiatives to address social issues and contribute to the betterment of the community.

Social responsibility Civic awareness Community service Leadership qualities Social harmony Student participation
Our Mission

To develop responsible citizens through community service.

NSS encourages students to understand society, identify community needs, participate in problem-solving and utilize their knowledge for practical solutions to individual and community problems.

Community involvement
Practical problem solving
Leadership development
Social harmony

NSS Motto

“Not Me But You”

The motto reflects democratic living and the need for selfless service towards society.

Objectives

Core objectives of NSS volunteers.

Understand Community

To understand the community in which students work.

Identify Problems

To identify the needs and problems of the community.

Civic Responsibility

To develop social and civic responsibility among students.

Practical Solutions

To use knowledge in finding practical solutions to problems.

Group Living

To develop competence required for group living and sharing responsibilities.

Mobilization Skills

To gain skills in mobilizing community participation.

Leadership

To acquire leadership qualities and democratic attitude.

National Integration

To practice national integration and social harmony.

Benefits of Joining NSS

Why students should join NSS.

Gain Life Skills

Develop confidence, teamwork, communication and leadership qualities.

Make Social Impact

Contribute positively to society and local communities.

Build Network

Connect with like-minded individuals and active volunteers.

Service Experience

Participate in structured social service and awareness programmes.

Activities & Events

NSS programmes and community activities.

Blood Donation Camps

Health Awareness Campaigns

Campaigns related to community health and public awareness.

Voting Awareness Campaign
